Global benchmarks

Every scan checks five of
the world's strictest regulators.

We cross-reference what you're buying against EU EFSA, US FDA, WHO Codex, Health Canada and UK FSA simultaneously and show you where FSSAI differs.

EU EFSA
European Food Safety Authority. The strictest food and cosmetic standards in the world. Bans over 1,600 substances permitted nowhere else.
US FDA
Food and Drug Administration. Governs food additives, cosmetic safety and pharmaceutical ingredients across the United States.
WHO Codex
Codex Alimentarius. The international baseline for food safety, pesticide residues and contaminant limits across 188 member countries.
Health Canada
One of the world's most conservative regulators. Particularly strict on cosmetics, baby products and food additives.
UK FSA
UK Food Standards Agency. Post-Brexit framework that in several categories exceeds even EU standards on contaminants and additives.
FSSAI
India's food regulator. Shown where standards differ. Labelyze tells you when a product meets international thresholds but not local ones, and vice versa.

Our mission

We built this because
the information gap is real.

Consumers in Europe and North America have access to detailed ingredient safety data at the point of purchase. In India, that same information exists. It is just never been made easy to use when it actually matters.

Labelyze does not make decisions for you. It gives you the facts, cites the source, and lets you decide. We are not anti-brand. We are pro-information.

Everything we flag is referenced to a published regulatory source. If we raise a concern, we tell you exactly which body raised it, when, and why. No black-box scores. No unexplained warnings. Just clear information, when you need it.
