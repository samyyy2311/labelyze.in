Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026 · Built by CaffeineLabs, India
Overview

Labelyze is built on a single privacy principle: your personal data belongs on your device, not on our servers. This policy explains precisely what we collect, what we do not collect, and why the architecture makes that guarantee structural rather than just a policy promise.

1. What we collect in the app

Nothing. The Labelyze app collects no personal data from you. There is no account, no login, no registration, and no user identifier of any kind. Specifically:

  • Your scan history is stored only in local device storage and never transmitted anywhere
  • Your health conditions, dietary preferences, and food goals are stored only on your device
  • Your ingredient watchlist and all profile settings are device-local only
  • Your Rewyr Score, streaks, and pattern data never leave your phone
  • Scan history exports are triggered entirely by you and delivered directly to your device share sheet
2. What we collect on this website

If you sign up for launch updates on this website, we collect your email address only. It is used solely to notify you about Labelyze. We do not sell, rent, or share it with any third party. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email we send.

This website does not use tracking cookies, third-party analytics scripts, advertising pixels, or behavioural tracking of any kind.

3. Product database and Open Food Facts

Labelyze uses product data from Open Food Facts, licensed under the Open Database License (ODbL). When your device queries this data, it queries a local SQLite database bundled with the app. No query leaves your device for routine scans.

For barcodes not in the local database, the app may query the Open Food Facts REST API. This sends only the barcode number — no personal data, no health profile, no identity information.

Labelyze is not affiliated with or endorsed by Open Food Facts.

4. Product submissions

When a product is missing from the database, you can submit it voluntarily. Before any submission is sent, you are shown exactly what will be shared and asked to confirm. Specifically:

  • Submissions include only the product name, brand, ingredient text, and an optional photo
  • You choose at submission time whether to share anonymously with Open Food Facts as well
  • The default for this choice is set by your global sharing preference in Profile, which you control
  • Submissions made while offline are queued locally and retried automatically
  • Submitted product data may become publicly visible via Open Food Facts under the ODbL licence
5. Community notes

The Community tab allows you to view and submit product observations. Submitted notes are associated with the product barcode only, not with any personal identifier. Do not include personal information in community submissions.

6. Weekly summary notifications

Labelyze offers an optional Sunday digest notification summarising your week's scans. This is opt-in, processed entirely on-device, and never transmitted to us. You can disable it at any time from Profile.

7. Advertising and third-party SDKs

Labelyze contains no advertising. There are no advertising SDKs, no ad networks, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no crash reporting tools in the app at launch. If we add crash reporting in a future version, it will be disclosed in an updated policy and you will be notified.

8. DPDP Act 2023 compliance

Labelyze is designed to comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. We collect only the minimum data necessary, for the specific purpose stated, and do not retain it beyond that purpose. Because the app processes all personal data on-device, the Act's data fiduciary obligations apply only to the limited email data collected via this website.

9. Children's privacy

Labelyze is a general audience app. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app requires no account and collects no personal data, which means children can use it without any data being transmitted. If you believe a child has submitted personal data via this website, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Data retention

App data: retained on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. No copy exists on our servers.

Website email signups: retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

Product submissions: once shared with Open Food Facts under ODbL, that data is governed by their terms. Our copy is retained for database improvement only and is not linked to you personally.

11. Your rights

Because the app stores no personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf for app usage. For website email data, you have the right to access, correct, or delete it at any time by contacting us below.

12. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify you via the app or by email if you have subscribed. The latest version is always available at labelyze.in.

13. Contact

Our mission

We built this because
the information gap is real.

Consumers in Europe and North America have access to detailed ingredient safety data at the point of purchase. In India, that same information exists. It has just never been easy to access at the moment you actually need it.

Labelyze does not make decisions for you. It gives you the facts, cites the source, and lets you decide. We are not anti-brand. We are pro-information.

Everything we flag is referenced to a published regulatory source. If we raise a concern, we tell you exactly which body raised it, when, and why. No unexplained scores. No vague warnings. Just clear information, when you need it.
