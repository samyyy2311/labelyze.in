Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 April 2026
What we collect

Labelyze does not collect any personal information. There is no account, no login, and no profile linked to you.

Your scan history, health profile, allergen selections, dietary preferences, ingredient watchlist, personal notes, and weekly goal are all stored locally on your device. None of this data is transmitted to any server.

Product submissions

If you choose to submit a missing product, you are sharing: the product name, brand, barcode, category, ingredients, and optionally nutrition information. This is submitted to a database managed by CaffeineLabs for the purpose of improving the product database. Submissions are opt-in only. You can turn off sharing at any time in Profile > Data and Privacy, or during onboarding.

When sharing is turned off, no data from your device is sent anywhere.

Analytics and advertising

Labelyze contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers of any kind.

Notifications

If you opt in to the weekly summary notification, this is scheduled locally on your device. No data is sent to a server to trigger it.

App review prompt

The app may ask you to rate it on the Play Store using the standard in-app review prompt. This prompt is provided by Google. No data is collected or shared by Labelyze as part of this prompt.

Children

Labelyze does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. No personal data is collected at all.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at labelyze.in/privacy with a new effective date.

Contact

For any privacy questions: hello@labelyze.in

Our mission

We built this because
the information gap is real.

Consumers in Europe and North America have access to detailed ingredient safety data at the point of purchase. In India, that same information exists. It has just never been easy to access at the moment you actually need it.

Labelyze does not make decisions for you. It gives you the facts, cites the source, and lets you decide. We are not anti-brand. We are pro-information.

Everything we flag is referenced to a published regulatory source. If we raise a concern, we tell you exactly which body raised it, when, and why. No unexplained scores. No vague warnings. Just clear information, when you need it.
