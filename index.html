Global benchmarks

Five frameworks.
One scan.

Every product you scan is cross-referenced against the world's most rigorous regulatory bodies simultaneously. FSSAI is shown where standards differ.

EU EFSA
European Food Safety Authority. The strictest food and cosmetic standards in the world. Bans over 1,600 substances permitted nowhere else.
US FDA
Food and Drug Administration. Governs food additives, cosmetic safety and pharmaceutical ingredients across the United States.
WHO Codex
Codex Alimentarius. The international baseline for food safety, pesticide residues and contaminant limits across 188 member countries.
Health Canada
One of the world's most conservative regulators. Particularly strict on cosmetics, baby products and food additives.
UK FSA
UK Food Standards Agency. Post-Brexit framework that in several categories exceeds even EU standards on contaminants and additives.
FSSAI
India's food regulator. Shown where standards differ. Labelyze tells you when a product meets international thresholds but not local ones, and vice versa.

Our mission

Built on one belief.

Indians deserve the same product safety information that consumers in Europe and North America take for granted. The data already exists. Every banned ingredient, every restricted substance, every recalled product is documented by regulators around the world. It is simply never made accessible to you at the moment you are standing in a store deciding what to buy.

Labelyze does not make judgements on your behalf. It gives you the facts, cites the source, and lets you decide. We are not anti-brand. We are pro-information.

Everything Labelyze tells you is referenced to a published regulatory source. If we flag something, we tell you exactly which body flagged it, when, and why. No algorithms deciding what is good for you. No scores without evidence. Just the information, in plain language, when you need it.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What we collect
We collect only your email address when you join the waitlist. We do not collect any health data, scan history, or personal information at this stage. The Labelyze app, once launched, is designed to process all personal data including health profiles, blood reports and scan history entirely on your device. None of this is ever transmitted to our servers.
2. How we use your email
Your email is used solely to notify you when Labelyze launches on Android. We will send one email at launch and occasional product updates if you choose to remain subscribed. We do not sell, rent, or share your email with any third party.
3. Data storage
Waitlist emails are stored securely using industry-standard practices. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing founder@labelyze.in.
4. Cookies
This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We do not use advertising networks or behavioural tracking of any kind.
5. India data protection compliance
Labelyze is designed to comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act). We collect only the minimum data necessary, process it only for the stated purpose, and do not retain it beyond what is required. The on-device architecture of the Labelyze app means that sensitive personal health data is never processed on external servers, aligning with the DPDP Act's data minimisation principles.
6. Your rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at founder@labelyze.in. We will respond within 30 days.
