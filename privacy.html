Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 April 2026 CaffeineLabs · India

Short version: Labelyze does not collect your personal data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. The only data that ever leaves your device is product information you choose to submit to help improve the ingredient database, and that is entirely opt-in.

Who we are

Labelyze is an Android application developed and operated by CaffeineLabs, an independent software studio based in India. The app helps users understand the ingredients in Indian packaged food products by cross-referencing them against international regulatory databases.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@labelyze.in.

What data we collect

Labelyze does not collect personal information. There is no account creation, no login, and no profile linked to you.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Scan history
  • Health profile and dietary conditions
  • Allergen selections
  • Ingredient watchlist
  • Personal notes on products
  • Weekly goal settings

This data exists only on your phone. Deleting the app removes it entirely.

Product submissions

If you choose to submit a missing product, the following information is shared with CaffeineLabs:

  • Product name and brand
  • Barcode number
  • Product category
  • Ingredient list
  • Nutrition information (optional)

This information is used solely to improve the Labelyze product database. Submissions are entirely opt-in. You can disable sharing at any time from Profile > Data and Privacy, or during the initial onboarding flow.

When sharing is turned off, no data from your device is transmitted anywhere.

Analytics and advertising

Labelyze contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no third-party tracking of any kind. We do not know how you use the app and we do not sell data.

Notifications

If you opt into the weekly summary notification, it is scheduled locally on your device using Android's notification scheduler. No data is sent to an external server to trigger it.

In-app review prompt

The app may ask you to rate it on the Google Play Store using the standard Google in-app review API. This prompt is provided and controlled by Google. Labelyze does not collect or receive any data as part of this interaction.

Camera permission

The app requests camera access to scan barcodes and ingredient labels. Camera data is processed entirely on-device. No images or camera output are stored, transmitted, or shared.

Children

Labelyze does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. As no personal data is collected from any user, this applies universally.

Data retention and deletion

Because all personal data is stored locally on your device, you control it entirely. Clearing app data or uninstalling the app removes everything. There is no server-side account to delete.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be published at labelyze.in/privacy with a new effective date. The app will notify you of significant changes on the next launch.
